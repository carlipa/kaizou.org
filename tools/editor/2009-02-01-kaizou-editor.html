Ever wanted to have a really customized web experience ? Kaizou is just for you ! With Kaizou, you can edit any online web page, save your changes to your local disk and share them with your friends. Don't see the point ? Click on the link below to have a sneak preview of what Kaizou can do :

Still not convinced ? You can use Kaizou to :
  • provide an unofficial revamping of a specific web page,
  • share your thoughts on a particular web page by commenting it,
  • apply a text converter to any page (like 'see the web as yoda does').

How does-it work ?

Kaizou-links or "klinks" are special links that can be embedded in Web pages. Clicking on a Klink will open the corresponding target page and apply the Kaizou modifications to it. Klinks are actually pointing at files based on a specific description format based on XML. Note: Kaizou offers editing tools for those who don't speak XML fluently.
